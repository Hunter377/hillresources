Welcome Home

Our homes are located in safe, family-friendly neighborhoods

and are maintained with 24/7 support staff. Our experienced and highly trained staff provide residents with the assistance they need. From learning how to wash their clothes to obtaining a driver’s license, individuals are provided the supports they need whether in a group home or host home option.

"Hill Resources is my home and my family. There is nowhere else I’d rather be!" – Sara (Resident)

Farm Life

Culpepper Creek Farm is our innovative

green, 3-5 sizes larger) day program where individuals come to learn and experience the art of growing plants in a greenhouse setting. When not in the greenhouses, individuals enjoy taking care of our chickens and miniature horses as well as growing various vegetable and herbs in our raised gardens. This peaceful setting is a popular choice for our residents.

Years of Leadership

A team grounded in love

Since 1991, our programs have been serving and empowering those with intellectual and developmental disabilities. Our team, from direct support staff to senior administrative roles, passionately serve our residents and share a commitment to enriching the lives of those who trust us on a daily basis. We believe God has a plan for everyone. It’s been amazing to see how God has used those with Intellectual and Developmental Disabilities to teach others about real and lasting love.

Improved Quality of Life through Technology

Wow, what a difference our technology makes in the loves of those we serve and our team members!

Less paper equals more people time, medical alerts, emergency reports … translates to proven better care! We and our team of IDD industry experts have developed Taskmaster Pro into the leading IDD electronic health record used in the state of texas.
